Acne is one of the most common skin concerns affecting Nigerians of all ages. Nigeria's hot, humid climate, combined with dietary factors and hormonal influences, creates the perfect environment for acne to thrive. The good news? Modern aesthetic medicine now offers a range of highly effective treatments that can clear even the most stubborn breakouts and dramatically reduce acne scarring.

In this expert guide, our certified aestheticians at Infinity Luxury Spa break down the best acne treatments available in Nigeria in 2026 — from professional spa treatments to at-home care — so you can finally achieve the clear, glowing skin you deserve.

Understanding Acne: Why Nigerian Skin is Particularly Vulnerable

Before diving into treatments, it's important to understand why acne is so prevalent in Nigeria. Several factors contribute:

  • Climate: Nigeria's heat and humidity cause increased sebum (oil) production and sweat, which clog pores
  • Diet: High-glycaemic foods common in Nigerian cuisine can trigger hormonal fluctuations that worsen acne
  • Water quality: Hard water in some Nigerian cities can strip the skin's protective barrier
  • Hormonal factors: Particularly relevant for women, hormonal acne is extremely common and often under-treated
  • Product sensitivity: Many imported skincare products are not formulated for darker skin types and can cause irritation-related breakouts

Professional Acne Treatments Available at Infinity Luxury Spa

1. HydraFacial for Acne (₦85,000)

The HydraFacial is one of our most effective tools for acne-prone skin. The treatment uses salicylic acid to dissolve excess oil, gently extracts pore-clogging impurities, and delivers anti-inflammatory, hydrating serums that calm active breakouts without irritation. Results are visible after just one session — and unlike some acne treatments, there is zero downtime.

For acne-prone clients, we customize the HydraFacial serum selection to include clarifying and sebum-regulating boosters for enhanced clearing action.

2. Chemical Peels (₦100,000)

Chemical peels are one of the most powerful professional acne treatments available. By applying a controlled acidic solution to the skin, we remove the outer layers of dead skin cells, unclog pores, reduce excess oil production, and fade post-acne hyperpigmentation — all in one treatment.

At Infinity Luxury Spa, we offer several types of chemical peels calibrated to your skin's specific needs. For active acne, we use salicylic acid-based peels. For acne scars and hyperpigmentation, we use glycolic or TCA-based peels. A series of 4–6 peels is typically recommended for optimal results.

3. Algae Peel (₦120,000)

Our signature Algae Peel is a biological exfoliation treatment using natural marine algae extracts. Unlike chemical peels, the algae peel is extremely gentle and suitable even for sensitive, acne-prone skin. It removes dead skin cells, purifies pores, reduces inflammation, and delivers powerful antioxidants that support skin healing.

The Algae Peel is particularly effective for clients who want the benefits of a peel without the downtime or sensitivity associated with chemical options.

4. Microneedling (₦80,000)

Microneedling — also known as Collagen Induction Therapy — is one of the most effective treatments for acne scars. Tiny, controlled micro-injuries stimulate the skin's natural healing response, triggering collagen and elastin production that fills in and smooths acne scars from within.

For active acne, microneedling creates micro-channels that allow topical anti-acne serums to penetrate much deeper into the skin than they can on their own — dramatically increasing their effectiveness. Results improve progressively over 3–6 months.

5. Laser Facial (₦100,000)

Our medical-grade laser facial targets multiple acne concerns simultaneously: it kills the P. acnes bacteria that cause breakouts, reduces sebum production from overactive oil glands, calms skin inflammation, and stimulates collagen to improve acne scarring — all in one treatment.

Our laser is specifically calibrated for Nigerian skin tones, ensuring safe, effective treatment with no risk of hyperpigmentation. Most clients require 4–6 sessions for optimal clearing.

6. Acne Scar Removal Treatment (₦60,000)

For clients whose primary concern is acne scarring rather than active breakouts, our dedicated Acne Scar Removal Treatment combines professional exfoliation, targeted scar-fading serums, and skin-regenerating technology to visibly reduce the appearance of post-acne marks, pitted scars, and hyperpigmentation.

7. PRP Therapy for Acne Scars (From ₦100,000)

For deep or stubborn acne scars, Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy is one of the most powerful treatments available. Growth factors in your own platelet-rich plasma stimulate intense collagen production and cellular renewal, visibly improving even deep pitted scars over a series of sessions.

When combined with microneedling — a treatment we call "Vampire Facial with PRP" — the results for acne scars are remarkable. Many clients see up to 70–80% improvement in scar appearance after 4–6 sessions.

At-Home Acne Care: What Our Aestheticians Recommend

Professional treatments work best when supported by a consistent at-home routine. Our aestheticians recommend:

  • Gentle, non-stripping cleanser: Cleanse twice daily with a pH-balanced cleanser specifically formulated for acne-prone skin
  • Niacinamide serum: A powerful ingredient that reduces sebum, fades hyperpigmentation, and strengthens the skin barrier
  • Oil-free, non-comedogenic moisturizer: Even oily, acne-prone skin needs hydration — look for lightweight, water-based formulas
  • Daily SPF 30–50: Sun protection is non-negotiable, especially in Nigeria's sun-intense climate, and is essential for preventing post-acne hyperpigmentation from darkening
  • Avoid touching your face: Hands carry bacteria that can transfer to your face and worsen breakouts

How Many Sessions Do I Need?

The number of sessions required depends on your acne severity and primary concern:

  • Mild acne and blackheads: 2–4 HydraFacial or chemical peel sessions
  • Moderate acne: 4–6 sessions of chemical peels or laser facial
  • Severe acne: 6–8 sessions combining laser, peels and targeted facials
  • Acne scars: 4–8 sessions of microneedling, PRP, or laser depending on severity
