Who We Are

Beauty Rooted in
Heritage & Science

Infinity Luxury Spa & Aesthetics is Northern Nigeria's most trusted luxury beauty and wellness brand — a place where African spa heritage meets modern aesthetic medicine.

Founded by certified aesthetician and beauty entrepreneur Aisha Hamza Khaleed, we opened our doors with a single vision: to give every Nigerian woman access to world-class beauty treatments in an environment that feels as luxurious as it looks.

Today, we operate across four cities — Kano, Abuja, Kaduna, and Sokoto — offering 50+ services from traditional African body rituals to cutting-edge procedures like HydraFacial, PRP Therapy, Laser Treatments, and IV Infusion Drips.

Meet the Visionary

Driven by Passion
for Beauty

"Every woman deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and well cared for — regardless of where she lives in Nigeria."

Aisha Hamza Khaleed is a certified beauty therapist, aesthetician, and serial entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in luxury spa management and aesthetic medicine. She founded Infinity Luxury Spa with the mission to bring globally competitive spa standards to Northern Nigerian cities.

She is also the founder of Tukunyar Kaka — a renowned herbal line dedicated to intimate wellness, infection care, and natural body enhancement. Her work bridges traditional African herbal knowledge with modern aesthetic science.

Our Journey

A Story of Growth

From a single location to four cities — our journey in numbers and milestones.

2019

The Beginning

Infinity Luxury Spa opens its first location in Kano, Nigeria — offering premium facial treatments and traditional spa services to an underserved market.

2020

Expanding Services

Introduction of advanced aesthetic treatments including IV Infusion Therapy, chemical peels, and body sculpting — bringing medical-grade aesthetics to Northern Nigeria.

2021

Abuja Launch

We opened our Abuja branch at Poly Plaza, Wuse 2 — establishing a presence in Nigeria's capital city and expanding our reach to a national audience.

2022

Kaduna & Sokoto

With growing demand across the North, we launched our Kaduna and Sokoto branches — completing our four-city footprint across Northern Nigeria.

2023

Skills Academy Launch

Infinity Skills Acquisition Academy opens — training the next generation of certified spa professionals. The Pedicure Masterclass becomes our flagship course.

2024–2026

Nigeria's Premier Luxury Spa

23,000+ social media followers, 5,000+ satisfied clients, and continued expansion of our service menu — now offering 50+ premium treatments across all branches.
