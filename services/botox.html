At Infinity Luxury Spa, our philosophy is simple: Botox and fillers should enhance your natural beauty — never change who you are. Our certified practitioners are trained in the most advanced injection techniques to deliver subtle, natural-looking results that make you look like the best version of yourself.

Botox (Botulinum Toxin) safely relaxes overactive facial muscles to smooth dynamic wrinkles like forehead lines, frown lines and crow's feet — with results lasting 3–6 months. Dermal Fillers add volume and definition to areas like lips, cheeks and chin — for an instantly more youthful, lifted appearance.

Every treatment begins with a thorough consultation to understand your goals and design a personalized treatment plan. Safety and natural results are our absolute priority.

Botox & Fillers FAQ

Botox results typically last 3–6 months, depending on the treatment area, the amount used, and individual metabolism. With regular maintenance treatments, many clients find their results last longer over time.
Yes, when administered by trained and certified professionals, Botox is extremely safe. At Infinity Luxury Spa, all injectable treatments are performed by certified aestheticians using pharmaceutical-grade products. We conduct a full health assessment before every treatment.
Hyaluronic acid lip fillers typically last 6–12 months. Results vary based on the type of filler, the amount used, and individual factors. Touch-up sessions can maintain your desired look.
Our approach prioritizes natural-looking enhancement. We use advanced injection techniques and premium products to deliver subtle, balanced results. During your consultation, we discuss your goals carefully to ensure you're completely happy with your treatment plan before we begin.
Minimal downtime. Some mild redness, swelling or bruising at the injection site is normal and resolves within 24–72 hours. Most clients return to normal activities the same day. We advise avoiding strenuous exercise and alcohol for 24 hours post-treatment.
