Why HydraFacial?

HydraFacial is the world's most celebrated facial treatment — and for good reason. Using patented Vortex-Fusion technology, it simultaneously cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and delivers potent serums deep into your skin.

Unlike traditional facials that can leave skin red and sensitive, HydraFacial is gentle enough for all skin types, including sensitive skin — with zero downtime. You'll see and feel the difference immediately after your very first session.

At Infinity Luxury Spa, our certified aestheticians customize every HydraFacial to your unique skin concerns — whether you're targeting acne, hyperpigmentation, anti-aging, or simply want that unmistakable HydraFacial glow.
