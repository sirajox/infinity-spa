Body Sculpting FAQ

Most clients need a minimum of 4–6 sessions for optimal fat reduction results. The exact number depends on your treatment area, the amount of fat to be reduced, and your individual body response. We design a personalized program during your consultation.
Lipolysis injections involve a mild stinging sensation at the injection site, which resolves quickly. Cavitation is completely painless — you'll hear a high-pitched tone during the treatment. Most clients find our body sculpting sessions very comfortable.
Some clients see initial changes after 2–3 sessions. Full results are typically visible 6–8 weeks after completing a course of treatment, as your body continues to process and eliminate the treated fat cells.
Lipolysis permanently destroys fat cells in the treated area. However, remaining fat cells can still expand with weight gain. Maintaining results requires a healthy lifestyle. Our team can advise on post-treatment maintenance for lasting results.
