Technology for Every Concern

Laser treatments represent the gold standard of skin correction technology. Using targeted beams of light energy, our advanced laser systems can address a wide range of skin concerns — from stubborn stretch marks and tribal marks to laser hair removal and intimate rejuvenation — with precision that no other treatment can match.

At Infinity Luxury Spa, we use medical-grade laser equipment operated by trained professionals. Every laser treatment is preceded by a detailed skin assessment to ensure safety, optimize settings for your skin type, and deliver the best possible results.

We are particularly proud to offer one of Nigeria's most comprehensive laser menus — including CO2 Laser Vagina Rejuvenation, a life-changing treatment that many of our clients travel across Nigeria to access.

Laser Treatment FAQ

Yes! Our laser systems are specifically selected and calibrated for darker skin tones, which are common across Nigeria. Our certified laser technicians are trained in treating all skin phototypes safely. We conduct a patch test before full treatment to ensure optimal safety and results.
Stretch mark removal typically requires 4–8 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart. Older, white stretch marks tend to require more sessions than newer, red or purple ones. Significant improvement is visible after 3–4 sessions.
Laser treatment can significantly fade and in many cases completely remove tribal marks, depending on their depth and age. Multiple sessions are required. Our team will give you a realistic assessment during your consultation based on your specific marks.
CO2 Laser Vagina Rejuvenation is a non-surgical treatment that uses fractional CO2 laser energy to tighten, tone and rejuvenate vaginal tissue. It addresses concerns like vaginal laxity, dryness, incontinence and discomfort — restoring comfort and confidence with minimal downtime.
Downtime varies by treatment. Laser facial: 2–5 days of redness/peeling. Stretch mark laser: mild redness for 1–3 days. Laser hair removal: minimal redness that clears in hours. CO2 intimate laser: 3–7 days of mild discomfort. Our team will give full aftercare guidance.
