Massage FAQ

Swedish Massage uses long, flowing strokes with light-to-medium pressure — perfect for relaxation, stress relief and improved circulation. Deep Tissue Massage targets deeper muscle layers with firmer pressure, ideal for chronic muscle tension, sports recovery and pain relief.
Lymphatic Drainage is a specialized gentle massage that stimulates the lymphatic system to remove toxins, reduce water retention and swelling, and boost immune function. It's particularly effective post-surgery, for clients with bloating, fatigue or those looking to detox the body.
Yes! Our Pregnancy Massage is specifically adapted to be safe and beneficial during pregnancy (second and third trimester). It helps relieve back pain, reduce swelling, improve sleep and ease anxiety. Please inform us of your pregnancy stage when booking.
For general wellness and stress management, once or twice a month is ideal. For specific therapeutic goals like pain relief or post-surgery recovery, more frequent sessions may be recommended by your therapist.
