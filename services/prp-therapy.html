PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy is one of the most scientifically advanced regenerative treatments available today. By using a concentrated sample of your own blood plasma — rich in growth factors and platelets — we stimulate your skin's natural repair and renewal process.

At Infinity Luxury Spa, we offer PRP for face (the famous Vampire Facial), hair restoration, body treatments, and our advanced O-Shot for intimate rejuvenation. PRP FAQ

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy involves drawing a small amount of your blood, spinning it in a centrifuge to concentrate the platelets and growth factors, then injecting or applying this rich plasma to the treatment area. These growth factors stimulate collagen production and cellular renewal naturally.
A topical numbing cream is applied before treatment to minimize discomfort. Most clients describe a mild pressure or tingling sensation during the procedure. Any redness or swelling typically resolves within 24–48 hours.
For optimal results, we recommend 3–4 sessions spaced 4 weeks apart, followed by maintenance sessions every 6–12 months. For hair loss treatment, 4–6 sessions may be needed initially.
Exosome Therapy uses nano-sized vesicles derived from stem cells to deliver powerful regenerative signals to your skin cells. It's considered more potent than traditional PRP, with more growth factors, faster results, and no need for blood draw. It's ideal for advanced anti-ageing and skin renewal.
Yes! PRP Hair Treatment is one of our most requested services. By injecting growth-factor-rich plasma into the scalp, we stimulate dormant hair follicles, improve hair density, and slow down hair loss. Most clients see visible improvement in 3–6 months.
